How do contemporary design principles differ from modern architecture? I'm having trouble distinguishing between the two.

Zaki (Instructor)

Great question, Emma! While modern architecture refers specifically to the early-to-mid 20th century movement (think Bauhaus, Le Corbusier), contemporary architecture is more fluid and refers to designs of the present day that incorporate current styles, technologies, and materials.
